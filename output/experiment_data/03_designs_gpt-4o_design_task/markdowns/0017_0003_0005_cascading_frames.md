## Interpretation  
  
### Implications_form :  
The metaphor &#x27;Cascading frames&#x27; implies a building design that features a series of frames or layers that progressively change in size or orientation, creating a sense of dynamic movement and depth. The massing could be composed of overlapping or intersecting frames that create a complex, multi-dimensional facade. This layered composition emphasizes verticality while allowing for horizontal connections between spaces. The spatial relationships between frames are organized to create a flowing sequence of spaces that guide the observer&#x27;s journey through the building, enhancing the vertical and horizontal connectivity. The geometry might incorporate curved or angled elements to enhance the cascading effect, with each frame casting shadows on the adjacent ones to create a rich interplay of light and shadow, accentuating the depth and rhythm of the design.  
### Metaphor :  
Cascading frames  
### Key_traits :  
This metaphor evokes a sense of dynamic progression and layered depth. It suggests a design where structural elements are organized in successive tiers, creating an interplay of light and shadow. The cascading nature implies fluidity and movement, allowing for visual complexity and spatial continuity. These frames can guide the eye through a sequence of spaces, emphasizing verticality and connectivity within the architecture.  
### Design_task :  
Develop an Architectural Concept Model that embodies the &#x27;Cascading frames&#x27; metaphor by constructing a series of overlapping frames that vary in size and orientation to create a sense of movement and depth. Utilize different materials or colors to distinguish the layers and emphasize the cascading effect. Pay attention to how light and shadow interact across the frames, using perforations or cutouts to enhance the visual complexity. The model should suggest a fluid transition between spaces, with each frame serving as a spatial link that encourages exploration and connectivity. Consider how the cascading arrangement could respond to environmental conditions or views, reinforcing the metaphor&#x27;s emphasis on dynamic progression and layered depth.
