## Interpretation  
  
### Implications_form :  
The &#x27;rippled grid&#x27; metaphor influences the building&#x27;s form and massing by introducing rhythmic undulations to an otherwise organized and regular grid structure. This creates a visual and spatial dynamic that suggests movement and fluidity. The geometry of the building may feature wavy or curved surfaces that ripple across the facade, while maintaining a consistent underlying grid that organizes the overall layout. Spatial relationships could be defined by a series of interconnected spaces that follow the ripple pattern, creating a flow from one area to another while adhering to the grid&#x27;s structure.

rippled grid  
### Key_traits :  
The metaphor &#x27;rippled grid&#x27; suggests a dynamic and rhythmic spatial quality. It implies a structured yet fluid pattern reminiscent of waves or ripples that propagate across a uniform grid. This can translate into architectural designs that incorporate undulating surfaces or facades, creating a sense of movement and flow while maintaining an underlying order and regularity.
