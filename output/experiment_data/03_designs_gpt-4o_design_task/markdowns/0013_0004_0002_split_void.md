## Interpretation  
  
### Implications_form :  
The &#x27;Split void&#x27; metaphor introduces a concept of dividing space through an intentional void that shapes the building&#x27;s massing into two or more contrasting entities. This void acts not only as a separator but as an active participant in the building&#x27;s tectonics, influencing geometry and silhouette with a distinct division. The spatial relationships are characterized by a central void that organizes the flow and interaction of spaces, creating a dialogue between enclosed and open areas. The void can enhance the perception of openness and movement, providing varied light conditions and shadow play that adds depth and contrast. The building&#x27;s form may exhibit a tension between solid and void, with elements that seem to pull away or lean into the void, creating a dynamic visual and spatial experience.  
### Metaphor :  
Split void  
### Key_traits :  
The metaphor &#x27;Split void&#x27; implies a design characterized by a clear division or separation within a central open space. This can create a dynamic tension and a sense of duality or contrast within the architecture, allowing for varied interactions with light and shadow. It suggests the creation of distinct zones or pathways and can evoke feelings of openness and movement while maintaining a strong formal identity.  
### Design_task :  
Construct an Architectural Concept Model that manifests the &#x27;Split void&#x27; metaphor by emphasizing the interaction between solid and void. Design a structure where the void is not just a separator but a dynamic element that informs the building&#x27;s identity and spatial logic. Focus on the contrast in materiality or form across the void, perhaps using a curvilinear or faceted void to introduce complexity in the spatial experience. Consider how the void influences the perception of movement and flow within the building, creating pathways that traverse or engage with the void. Use the model to explore how the void can enhance interactions with natural elements like light and air, creating a vivid contrast and dynamic interplay that reflects the metaphor&#x27;s inherent tension and duality.
