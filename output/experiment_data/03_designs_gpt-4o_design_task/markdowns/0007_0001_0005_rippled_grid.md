## Interpretation  
  
### Implications_form :  
The &#x27;rippled grid&#x27; metaphor suggests a building form that combines the regularity and order of a grid with the dynamic, undulating qualities of ripples. The massing could feature a facade or roofline that appears to wave or ripple, introducing movement and rhythm into the structure. Spatially, this could translate into interior spaces that flow and connect in a wave-like manner, while still adhering to an underlying grid system. The building&#x27;s silhouette might undulate, creating a sense of fluidity while maintaining a structured base.  
### Metaphor :  
rippled grid  
### Key_traits :  
The metaphor &#x27;rippled grid&#x27; suggests a dynamic and rhythmic spatial quality. It implies a structured yet fluid pattern reminiscent of waves or ripples that propagate across a uniform grid. This can translate into architectural designs that incorporate undulating surfaces or facades, creating a sense of movement and flow while maintaining an underlying order and regularity.

The model should explore how these ripples can influence both the exterior form and the interior spatial relationships, maintaining a balance between fluidity and order. Consider how light interacts with the rippled surfaces, enhancing the dynamic quality of the design.
