## Interpretation  
  
### Implications_form :  
The metaphor of &#x27;Interlocking Layers&#x27; suggests a building form where multiple planes or volumes overlap and integrate, forming a rich tapestry of spatial interaction. This approach influences the massing by creating a composition that is both complex and cohesive, with a silhouette defined by interwoven elements that rise and recede. Spatially, the interlocking layers imply a design where spaces are interconnected yet distinct, enabling a fluid transition between various functional areas while maintaining privacy and openness. The arrangement encourages a balance between integrated and discrete experiences, with layers serving as connectors and partitions that shape the user journey through the architecture.  
### Metaphor :  
Interlocking Layers  
### Key_traits :  
This metaphor suggests a design characterized by overlapping and interconnected planes or volumes. The interlocking nature creates dynamic spatial relationships and visual depth, allowing for both openness and separation within the architecture. It emphasizes a structural and spatial complexity, where different layers interact to provide variety in function and experience.  
### Design_task :  
To realize the &#x27;Interlocking Layers&#x27; metaphor in an Architectural Concept Model, construct a series of overlaid and integrated planes or volumes that illustrate the concept of interaction and connectivity. Utilize materials with varying textures and finishes to differentiate the layers, highlighting their individual roles while showcasing their cohesion in the overall structure. Focus on creating a model that emphasizes the interplay between open and enclosed spaces, illustrating how the layers can simultaneously provide separation and unity. Experiment with the arrangement and orientation of layers to capture the dynamic nature of their interaction, ensuring the model reflects the spatial complexity and depth inherent in the metaphor.
